2. Functional Group Priorities

Highest priority gets lowest carbon position number & dictates principal suffix:
1Carboxylic Acid (-COOH)-oic acid
2Aldehyde (-CHO)-al
3Ketone (-CO-)-one
4Alcohol (-OH)-ol
5Triple Bond (C≡C)-yne
6Double Bond (C=C)-ene
7Single Bond (C-C)-ane

3. Punctuation Assembly Rules

• Use hyphens (-) to separate numbers from text words (e.g., propan-2-ol).
• Use commas (,) to separate multiple numbers from each other (e.g., 2,2-dimethylpropane).
• Run words together into a single sequence without blank spaces.
